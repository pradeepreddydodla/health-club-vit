Our Events

  • THE PAROPKAR

    Paropkar is an event which is conducted every year during the last month of winter semester. The main motto of the event is to collect beds, books, buckets and clothes from the students who are getting graduated that year. After the termend exams, we distribute the collected stuff to various orphanages and homes depending on their requirements.

  • THE ANANDHAM

    Anandham is an event which was held on World Happiness Day. Our students graced the occasion with their performances, and spread smiles all around. Counselling division in collaboration with health club organises many other sub-events like dubsmash, treasure hunt and dance therapy as a part of world happiness day.

  • THE EYE CAMP

    The eyes are the windows of the soul. The Eye Camp is one of the most recent events that has been organised by Health Club VIT in the presence of ophthalmologists from vasan eye care in which more than 400+ participants took part. we conduct these camps because every little gesture by us would make a world of a difference to someone else.

  • OTHER EVENTS

    We empower communities, encourage good citizenship, tackle youth based issues such as Sexual Abuse and HIV, Substance Abuse, give young change makers a platform to lead. We also conduct many other health camps and awareness camps which include blood donation camp, dental camp, CPR workshops and special occasions like world mental health day, world suicide prevention day etc.

HEALTH CLUB




What we actually do:

The Health Club of VIT believes in the motto – ‘A Healthy Habit, a Healthy World’. We are a family of Titians’ who are working for poor, needy and the betterment of the society. We create awareness about health issues, conduct free medical camps inside and outside campus, conduct fund raising events and donate them to the needy, distribute the used things that students and faculties donate to the needy. We often visit orphanages and give inspiring speeches and conduct interactive sessions. We also celebrate special occasions like world happiness day and suicide prevention day along with the Counselling Division of VIT.


How it all started:

Health Club (VIT) was founded under the guidance of Chief Medical Officer, VIT Vellore and since then we have grown leaps and bounds. Our faculty coordinator is "Dr.Bangalore Morarji(Social Science Division,SSL)"
